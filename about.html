About Our Youth Group

We are the SVA Youth Group at Southern Virginia Baptist Church in Yorktown, Virginia. As believers in Christ, we gather to pray, grow in God’s Word, and share the gospel together.

Theme Verse

But reject profane and old wives' fables, and exercise yourself toward godliness. [1 Timothy 4:7]

What We Do

We meet every Sunday at 9:30 AM for small groups and regularly serve in both large and small church ministries. Students earn “talents” through faithful participation—attendance, TrueLight assignments, closing prayer, and offering. At year’s end, we celebrate with a Talent Market, a fun reminder that God rewards faithfulness.
